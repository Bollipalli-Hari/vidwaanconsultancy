Company Registration in India

We help you register your Private Limited or LLP company with end-to-end documentation and compliance support. Typical registration time: 7–10 working days (subject to MCA approvals).

Documents required for company incorporation

  • PAN card of all directors
  • Aadhaar card of all directors
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Address proof of directors (bank statement / electricity bill, etc.)
  • Registered office proof – rent agreement / sale deed
  • NOC from owner (if rented premises)
  • Latest electricity bill of office

Process & timeline

  1. Name reservation & DSC/DIN
  2. Drafting MOA & AOA
  3. Filing incorporation forms with MCA
  4. Receiving Certificate of Incorporation, PAN & TAN
