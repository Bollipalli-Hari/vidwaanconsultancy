Documents Required for Company Incorporation

For each Director / Shareholder

  • PAN card
  • Aadhaar card
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Address proof – bank statement / driving licence / voter ID
  • Mobile number & email ID

For Registered Office

  • Rent agreement / sale deed
  • Electricity bill or property tax receipt (recent)
  • NOC from owner of premises (if rented)

Don’t worry if any of these are not ready – our team will guide you on alternatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does company registration usually take?

Generally 7–10 working days after all documents are submitted, depending on name approval and MCA processing.

Is physical visit required?

No, the entire process is online. You only need to share documents and e-sign forms.

Can I use my home as registered office?

Yes, many startups use their home address initially. We just need basic address documents and owner NOC.
